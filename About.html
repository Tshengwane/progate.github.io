Tshengwane Kaletsane

Tshengwane Kaletsane has had a deep interest in politics since a young age, and through the use of ink as a medium, he is able to bring about awareness on the good and the bad of events cause by political influence in a form of art. His work mainly focuses on the standard of politics and social issues in the African continent. In his art, he portrays the relationship between leaders and civilians of countries. His work is viewed as both negative and positive, which gives the viewer an understanding of the two parties in play!

He also explores the events that happen in our daily lives. The negative and the positive!
